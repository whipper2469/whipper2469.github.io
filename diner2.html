Our Story

Joe's Diner started as a small family restaurant in 1978 with just ten tables. Founded by Joe and Mary Thompson, our mission was simple: serve delicious, home-style meals that make people feel at home.

Today, we're still family-owned and operated, with Joe's grandson now running the kitchen. While we've expanded over the years, we've never compromised on quality or that homemade taste that keeps our customers coming back.

We source locally whenever possible, supporting our community farmers and producers. Every dish is prepared fresh daily, from our signature pies to our famous meatloaf.
