How it works

Fixed in four steps

From problem to repair in minutes, not days.

1

Snap a photo

Take a photo of the issue — leaky pipe, broken outlet, cracked wall — and let the app identify what's wrong.

2

Get a diagnosis

Receive an instant assessment with estimated cost, urgency level, and recommended next steps.

3

Book a contractor

Connect with verified local contractors in your area who are ready to take the job — no cold calling required.

4

Track the repair

Follow the job through every stage — submitted, accepted, scheduled, in progress, and completed.
