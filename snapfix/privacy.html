SnapFix Inc. ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the SnapFix mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service"). Please read this policy carefully. By using SnapFix, you consent to the practices described herein.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Account Information: full name, email address, phone number, home address, postal code, and role (homeowner, tenant, landlord, or contractor).
  • Photos and Diagnosis Data: images you submit for AI diagnosis, issue descriptions, and diagnosis results generated by the App.
  • Repair Request Data: issue type, urgency level, contact details, and job history.
  • Technical Information: device type, operating system version, app version, and crash/error logs collected to improve stability.
  • Usage Data: features accessed and general usage patterns. No advertising profiles are built from this data.

We do not collect precise GPS location data. We only use the postal code you voluntarily provide to match you with nearby contractors.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide AI-assisted home repair diagnoses
  • Match you with local contractors based on your location and issue type
  • Process and display your repair requests to relevant contractors
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset, repair request updates)
  • Send a one-time welcome email upon registration
  • Improve, debug, and develop new features for the App
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do NOT use your data for advertising, behavioral tracking, or data brokering of any kind.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (PIPEDA)

We process your personal information under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable provincial privacy laws. Our legal bases are:

  • Consent: given when you create an account and agree to these terms.
  • Performance of a contract: to provide the services you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests: to improve the App and prevent fraud.
  • Legal obligation: where required by applicable law.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Supabase (supabase.com), a cloud database provider with servers in North America. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS for all data transmission)
  • Encryption at rest (AES-256)
  • Role-based access controls
  • Row-level security policies limiting data access strictly to authorized users

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted to or stored by the Service.

5. Sharing Your Information

We share your personal information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Contractors: when you request a quote, your name, phone number, and address may be shared with the contractor you select.
  • Service Providers: we use Supabase for database storage and Resend for transactional email delivery. These providers process data only as instructed by us and under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: we may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business Transfer: in the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred, with advance notice provided to you.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing or commercial purposes.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. If you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it for a longer period (e.g., financial records, legal disputes).

7. Your Rights

Under PIPEDA and applicable provincial law, you have the right to:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: withdraw consent and request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Complaint: file a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada at www.priv.gc.ca.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@snapfix.app. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Push Notifications

We may send push notifications about repair request updates and App features. You can disable push notifications at any time in your device's Settings app under Notifications → SnapFix.

9. Third-Party Links

The App and website may contain links to third-party websites or services (including contractor websites and Apple's App Store). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any third party. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing personal information.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to or intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us at support@snapfix.app.

11. Apple Platform Privacy

The App is distributed through the Apple App Store. We comply with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines regarding data collection and privacy disclosures, including providing an accurate privacy nutrition label in the App's App Store listing. Apple's own privacy practices are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/privacy.

We use the following device capabilities: Camera (for photo diagnosis), Microphone (for car noise diagnosis — optional feature only), and Photo Library (to upload existing photos). None of these are accessed without your explicit permission.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Sending an email to your registered address, and/or
  • Displaying a prominent notice in the App

Changes take effect 14 days after notification. Your continued use of the Service after that date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
