Everything You Need to Be Thoughtful

Cherish is designed to help you show up for your partner in meaningful ways, every single day.

👤

Partner Profile

Store important details like their birthday, anniversary, favorite flowers, restaurants, and gift ideas all in one place.

🌙

Date Night Ideas

Get bi-weekly date suggestions filtered by your budget and searchable near your location. Never run out of ideas.

🎬

Movies & Shows

Browse movies now playing with voting features and direct ticket links. Find comedy shows near you instantly.

🔔

Smart Reminders

Set random, weekly, or monthly reminders to do something thoughtful. Let Cherish keep you on track.

❄️

Cooling Off Mode

Step away gracefully with a 5-minute timer, get suggestions on making things right, and send pre-made messages.

🔒

Private & Secure

All your data stays on your device. No accounts required, no data collected, completely private.
