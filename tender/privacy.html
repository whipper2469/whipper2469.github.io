Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 18, 2026

Your Privacy Matters: Tender is designed with privacy at its core. All your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use Tender, you may choose to enter the following information:

  • Your partner's name, nickname, and important dates (birthday, anniversary)
  • Preferences such as favorite flowers, restaurants, and gift ideas
  • Custom reminders and notes

Important: All of this information is stored locally on your device using secure local storage. It is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

1.2 Location Information

Tender requests access to your location to provide the following features:

  • Finding date night ideas near you
  • Showing movies playing at nearby theaters
  • Discovering comedy shows in your area

Location data is used only to search for nearby venues and events. We do not store, track, or share your location data. You can control location permissions through your device settings at any time.

1.3 Notification Permissions

With your permission, Tender sends you reminders that you create within the app. These notifications are generated locally on your device and do not involve any external servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Because all data is stored locally on your device, we never have access to your information. The app uses your data exclusively for:

  • Displaying your partner's profile and preferences
  • Sending reminders you've set up
  • Searching for nearby venues and events based on your location
  • Providing personalized date night suggestions

3. Third-Party Services

Tender uses the following third-party APIs to provide certain features:

3.1 The Movie Database (TMDb)

We use TMDb's API to show movies currently playing in theaters. When you use the movie feature, your device makes requests directly to TMDb's servers. We do not send any of your personal information to TMDb. Please review TMDb's Privacy Policy for more information.

3.2 Ticketmaster

We use Ticketmaster's API to show comedy shows and live events near you. When you use this feature, your device makes requests directly to Ticketmaster's servers. We do not send any of your personal information to Ticketmaster. Please review Ticketmaster's Privacy Policy for more information.

4. Data Storage and Security

All of your data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by iOS. We employ industry-standard security practices to protect your information:

  • Data is encrypted using iOS's built-in security features
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No user accounts or login credentials are required

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored on your device:

  • You can delete any information at any time within the app
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data
  • We have no ability to recover your data once it's deleted, as we never had access to it

6. Children's Privacy

Tender is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has used our app, please contact us at kevin.8@icloud.com.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app at any time
  • Modification: You can edit or update any information in the app
  • Deletion: You can delete individual items or all data within the app
  • Location: You can disable location access in your device settings
  • Notifications: You can disable notifications in your device settings

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. International Users

Tender is available worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device, no data is transferred internationally. The app complies with local data protection laws by design.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: kevin.8@icloud.com
