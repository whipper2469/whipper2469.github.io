Tender is the relationship companion that sits quietly in your pocket, helping you be the partner you've always wanted to be. It remembers the details you might forget, suggests ideas when you're stuck, and gives you space to cool down when things get heated.

No social feeds. No judgment. No data leaving your device. Just a thoughtful nudge to show up with love, every day.

Why Tender?

Relationships thrive on the small things — remembering how they take their coffee, surprising them with flowers on a random Tuesday, or knowing exactly when to say "I'm sorry, let's talk about this."

But life gets busy. Calendars fill up, routines take over, and those small acts of love start slipping through the cracks. That's where Tender comes in.

Tender is not a dating app. It's not social media. It's a private, personal tool designed for people already in love who want to keep showing up. From organized partner profiles and curated date night ideas to smart reminders and a unique "Cooling Off" mode for those tough moments — Tender is your secret weapon for a stronger relationship.

Everything stays on your device. Your partner's birthday, their favorite restaurant, that inside joke only you two understand — it's all private, always. Because trust is the foundation of love.
