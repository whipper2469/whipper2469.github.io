About Babydoll

Babydoll Tattoos & Piercing is a queer-friendly, body-positive studio that believes every person deserves to feel at home in their skin.

Our artists specialize in working collaboratively with you — explaining every step, respecting your boundaries, and making sure you leave feeling seen, safe, and excited about your new piece.

“Your skin may be green, your skin may be blue. We're the ones they call different — but here? We're alien too.”
